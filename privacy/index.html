Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the BloomDrop app ("BloomDrop", "the app", "we") handles information. BloomDrop is made by an independent developer, Matan Keret. The short version: BloomDrop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

No data collection

BloomDrop does not collect personal information. Specifically:

  • No accounts. There is no sign-up, login, or user profile.
  • No third-party SDKs or ad networks. The app contains no advertising and no third-party software development kits.
  • No analytics or tracking. We do not use analytics, telemetry, fingerprinting, or any tracking technologies, and we do not track you across apps or websites.
  • No developer server. There is no developer-run server or backend. Your activity in the app is not sent to us.

Data stored on your device

BloomDrop saves your scores, progress, and saved games locally on your device so it can remember your best results, let you continue where you left off, and keep your settings. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

In a future update, BloomDrop may offer the option to sync your scores and progress across your own devices using Apple iCloud. If enabled, this uses Apple's iCloud service tied to your personal Apple Account — the data is handled by Apple under Apple's terms and privacy policy, and is not accessible to the developer. There is no developer-operated server involved.

Purchases

A future version of BloomDrop may include a one-time in-app purchase (using Apple's StoreKit) to unlock additional content. Any purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive or store your payment details. Purchase handling is governed by Apple's terms and privacy policy.

Children's privacy

BloomDrop does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children. Because the app collects no personal data and contains no ads, accounts, or third-party tracking, there is no personal information to gather from children or anyone else.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes (for example, when iCloud sync or an in-app purchase ships), we will update this page and revise the "last updated" date above. Material changes will continue to reflect the app's actual behavior.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact matan504@gmail.com.
