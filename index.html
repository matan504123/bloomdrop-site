A puzzler that paints itself

Simple to pick up, lovely to look at, and built to respect you.

Signature watercolor bleed

Every merge blooms and bleeds across the paper in soft, spreading washes — the visual heart of the game.

Drop & merge, up the ladder

Two same-color drops merge into the next color up a seven-tier pigment ladder. Easy to learn, hard to put down.

Endless escalating mode

The pace keeps rising and a creeping danger line keeps the pressure on. Relaxing — and quietly competitive.

Original art & music

Hand-tuned procedural watercolor art paired with an easygoing original soundtrack that never wears out its welcome.

Multiple color palettes

Repaint the whole game with selectable palettes — Watercolor, Twilight, and Sunset — to suit your mood.

Privacy-first & offline

No ads, no tracking, no accounts. Everything runs on your device and works fully offline.

How to play

Three steps, endless blooms.

1

Drop the pigment

Release a colored drop into the paper bin, choosing where it lands.

2

Match to merge

When two same-color drops touch, they merge into the next color up the ladder and bloom across the paper.

3

Stay under the line

Keep merging to make room. If the bin fills past the creeping danger line, the run ends — so beat your best.

Privacy by design

BloomDrop is built to be played, not to harvest your data. There's nothing to log in to and nowhere for your data to go.

  • No accounts, no sign-ups.
  • No third-party SDKs, ad networks, analytics, or tracking.
  • No personal data collected or transmitted.
  • Scores, progress, and saved games stay on your device.
  • No developer-run server or backend — ever.
